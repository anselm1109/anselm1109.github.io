Simply Pray

Welcome to Simply Pray.

This is a small app I created to help me in my Centering Prayer practice.

How to use:

  • Click or tap the right side of the screen to begin praying.
  • Use the gear icon to change settings and prayers.

Please note: In order for the prayer timer to work you must leave your phone or computer screen on.

Prayer of Intent

Prayer of Intent for Centering Prayer
    Father, Son and Holy Spirit,
    Creator, Redeemer and Sanctifier,
    alive at the center of our beings,
    we wish to surrender in love to You.
    May our sacred word,
    which we will gently pray when we engage with our thoughts,
    be a sign and symbol of our intention to consent to Your Divine Presence and Action within.
Source: COCI

Prayer Timer

Don't forget to:

  • Leave your screen on so the timer doesn't stop.
  • Silence your ringer and notifications.
  • Turn your media volume to a quiet, comfortable level.
  • iPhone users: if the timer is not appearing, try selecting a different amount of time in the settings and reloading by clicking the home button below. You should then be able to select any time you desire.
