Simply Pray

Welcome to Simply Pray.

This is a small project I created for my own use. It's not fancy, but it does what I want. I hope you find it helpful.

How to use:

  • Click or tap the right side of the screen to begin praying.
  • Click the gear icon to change the prayers and starting Psalm.
  • Settings are stored locally. Completely clear your browser's cache to remove them.
  • The daily Psalm will change every 22 hours or so.
  • The Prayer Book is only kinda working. Expect bugs.

Please note: In order for the prayer timer to work you must leave your phone or computer screen on.
