Welcoming Prayer

Adapted from Contemplative Outreach
1
Sink in to the present moment and what you are feeling right now in your body.
2
Welcome what you are feeling as an opportunity to consent to the Holy Spirit's indwelling.
3
Let go by saying “I let go of my desire for security, affection, control and embrace this moment as it is.”
